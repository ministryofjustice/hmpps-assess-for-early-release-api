Home Detention Curfew (tagging): Eligible

You are eligible for early release from prison on Home Detention Curfew (tagging).

What this means for you

This means you could be released from prison on 15 July 2026 if you:

  • behave well in prison; and
  • have somewhere to live after you leave prison where you can be safely managed.

If you are released early you would have to:

  • stay at home during certain house (your curfew); and
  • wear an electronic tag - to make sure you follow your curfew.
  • allow EMS provider staff access to your home to check the equipment.

What happens next

Think about where you could live if you were released on 15-July-2026. We will give you a form to fill in with the address details closer to the date you can be released on Home Detention Curfew.

If you can, think of a second address in case your first choice is not suitable.
